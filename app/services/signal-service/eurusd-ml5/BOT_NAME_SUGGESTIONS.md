## Naming Strategy Recommendations

### Option A: Branded Family Approach
Use one name for all bots with asset identifier:
- **EnsembleFX Pro - EUR/USD**
- **EnsembleFX Pro - GBP/JPY**
- **EnsembleFX Pro - USD/JPY**

**Pros**: Consistent branding, easy to scale
**Cons**: Less unique per asset

### Option B: Unique Names Per Asset
Each asset gets its own creative name:
- **EUR/USD**: "EuroEnsemble"
- **GBP/JPY**: "YenCollective"
- **USD/JPY**: "DollarQuintet"

**Pros**: More memorable, unique positioning
**Cons**: Harder to maintain brand consistency

### Option C: Descriptive + Asset
Technical name with asset:
- **ML5 Ensemble - EUR/USD**
- **ML5 Ensemble - GBP/JPY**
- **ML5 Ensemble - USD/JPY**

**Pros**: Clear what it is, easy to understand
**Cons**: Less creative, more generic
